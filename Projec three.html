Retro

This animated interactive artwork named Retro was coded with P5 Javascript. The motion of images "jitters" according to the rhythm of the sound in the environment. Users can click mouse to change the colour of background and press keys ‘a‘, ‘w‘, ‘d‘ and ‘f‘ to add sound effects.

User Interaction

  • Find the play button at the left buttom part (drag up the screen slightly if button is hidden).
  • Click mouse to view a different set of background animation.
  • Press keys 'a', 'w', 'd' and 'f' to play with sound effects and see how images change accordingly.
  • Experiment.
